PRODUCTS

We specialize in high fashion Ladies wear, Kids wear, fashion accessories in Woven and knitted fabrics. Apparel garments include: blouses, dresses, skirts, tops, jumper, kaftans, and maternity wear. You can have a look at some of our product samples online. Our value addition includes:

  • Processing: All kinds of dyeing, printing and finishes.
  • Hand Detailing: Pintucks, Beads Sequins, Studs, any other specialization.
  • Embroidery : Hand, chicken kari works, aari, chain, beading, fagotting, scalping, glass work, lucknowi works, machine & computer embroideries including Schiffly, Sequins works etc.
