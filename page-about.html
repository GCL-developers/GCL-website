Who are we ?

Who are we

General Commerce Ltd. started as a small company with nominal capital and employed less than 10 people but had the vision and the hard work of its founder, Mr. Yogeshwar Nath and late Shri Nath. Now General Commerce has a dedicated team of professionals and its own manufacturing facilities with latest Japanese machines from Brother and Juki to make world-class quality apparels. Since it is a family owned and run company, it values its commitment to its buyers and suppliers the most. This fact is reflected in its timely deliveries, close relationships with the suppliers and quick production turnaround.

GCL established in 1973, has more than 4 decades of experience in manufacturing and exportsof garments
