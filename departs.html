The mantra to a successful manufacturing company on a global platform is an efficient communication system. It is the people who create and maintain the line of communication who are important. Our merchandising team consists of 9-10 merchandisers who are responsible for the overall communication with buyers, from arranging meetings to listening and handling their customized orders. They are also responsible for developing new sample designs and executing the sample orders followed by the style costing of the product. Thus, planning, prioritizing, troubleshooting and getting the best goods across to our clients, that's what our merchandising team does the best. You can count on our team for all the information you need, well ahead of schedule, to help you plan your merchandising and sales.

Garment samples are inevitably important and are developed before starting the bulk production. We lay great importance to the product development as these samples are the only criterion which helps buyer decides the ability of an exporter. We provide our buyers the flexibility to place the order only after they are satisfied with the quality of our products. We guarantee to provide you our best innovative products and with optimum quality.

We have an in-house design studio which keeps on researching and developing new products according to the latest trend.

Our sampling department works closely with the merchandising department as merchandiser is the person who is interacting with the buyers regarding samples and other requirements. Merchandisers also advise sampling department suitably according to the requirements of the buyer such as price ranges and quality levels.

We work with our buyers continuously and keep on sending samples to them as per their requirement. Whenever they have enquiries and if they want to develop a new style in new fabric or want to see the garments in a new fabric, we provide them with all the required samples until they are completely satisfied with the final product.

Our Fabric store and Accessories store is an important component in the way our production wheel rotates. The entire stock of all fabrics and the accessories are maintained in a systematic manner for easy retrieval and processing. All the raw material or fabrics received at our stores are completely checked for consistency of quality and quantity. They are accepted only after passing the stringent quality and quantity audit.

The cutting section is equipped with the most modern CAD systems from Lectra used for Computerized Precision Pattern Making, Grading and Marking System. It improves turnaround times and expedites the cutting process with minimal waste and cost. The section also has Fusing machines. At this stage we also have an efficient bundling system that makes sure that the cut components are sent to the sewing lines in the manner most conducive to efficient and productive sewing output.

The sewing section has a hi-tech infrastructure with its wide range of advanced machinery & proficient labor capable of producing stylized garments with perfection & finesse. The production lines are fully integrated and operational with various types of powered sewing machines from Brother and Juki and other special machines.

The last stage after sewing involves packaging and presentation. We have state of the art steam ironing facilities with vacuum press tables. Great care is taken at this stage to ensure that any mistakes which have escaped our stringent quality control measures are rectified and the goods are accurate in terms of acceptable quality and quantity. The packaging is done according to the customer's preferences, including all labels, tags and other point of sale material as required. We ensure that all packing material used are compliant with global standards.

Being a quality-conscious organization, we have directed all careful efforts towards maintaining the high quality standards of our products. We take strict quality control measures to ensure that superior quality fabrics are used to design the products. In addition to this, our talented quality auditors thoroughly check the products at every level of production assuring that they are designed in sync with the international guidelines and norms. Our products are checked on regular intervals on the basis of its design, colour fastness, shrink resistance and its durability.
