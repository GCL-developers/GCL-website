OUR STRENGTH

A motivational team

Our greatest strength lies in the spirit of teamwork which always motivates us to work together towards a common vision. We have the capability to handle the most complex order through our streamlined work flow, from start to finish, under one roof.

Emphasis on TQM - Total Quality Management

The management at General Commerce Limited lays an extra emphasis on Total Quality management which helps in enhancing the quality and productivity in the organization. TQM helps us in building quality into the product at every stage. TQM has been successful at reducing costs and improving quality at the company.

Receptive to Newer Technology

In today's world, where every day newer technologies are emerging, We keep the attitude of accepting and working with the newer efficient technology to reach out, to make our customers feel informed and involved, and are committed to quality and timeliness. Our team keeps itself updated with new technologies be it in IT, ERP, Manufacturing, CAD or HR.

Timely Delivery

We, at GCL, ensure you the timely delivery of your order. We provide facilities to fulfill the smallest, most detailed requirements in a garment design with the discipline of accomplishing the time-line. We work with a highly trained and certified asset-based transportation firm. Timeline management helps us to keep the client informed of the status at various stages so that there are no last minute surprises.

Transparency and Open Communication

There is a transparent and open communication culture across the company, from suppliers to buyers, and among the company's employees. Communication encourages everyone to improve in specific areas and it shows commitment from each member working on the process.

In House Design Studio

The company has its own In-House Design Studio, where we create our own collection of ladies and kids wear inspired by the latest trends from Europe, the US and Asia. If you like something from our collection you can pick it out and add it to your new collection.
